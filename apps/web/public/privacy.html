Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MyFinanceOS. Your privacy is critically important to us. This application is designed with a "local-first, user-controlled" architecture. We do not run central databases that collect or store your personal financial data.

2. Data Collection and Storage

MyFinanceOS integrates with Google Drive APIs to store your financial data. All of your data (transactions, accounts, budgets, etc.) is saved directly to a hidden, secure folder in your own Google Drive account (the `appDataFolder`).

  • We do not have access to your Google Drive data.
  • We do not store your data on our own servers.
  • Your data never leaves your device except to sync directly to your personal Google Drive.

3. Authentication

We use Google OAuth for authentication to securely link the application to your Google Drive. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to read and write the application's configuration file within your Drive.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application is hosted on Vercel. Standard web hosting analytics (like IP addresses and user agents) may be temporarily logged by the hosting provider for security and performance monitoring. However, no personal financial information is ever transmitted to these servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer.
